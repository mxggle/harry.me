## **The Bite size Japanese Podcast**

最近真的好喜欢看这个小姐姐的Podcast. 语速不快，说话节奏很舒服。讲的都是自己亲身经历的一些小故事。

以下是他的频道介绍

みなさん、いつもPodcastを聞いてくれてありがとう！！  
I'm so happy to be a part of your Japanese learning journey…!!!  
I also used Podcasts to learn English and I've improved so much.

So now I really want to help Japanese learners to speak Japanese with my podcast!  
I want you to simply enjoy listening to Japanese because I believe you can learn so much more when you are just enjoying it and using Japanese just like a native speaker. I want to make this show feel like that!!

いつもありがとう！

Layla  
The Bite size Japanese Podcast

[The Bite size Japanese Podcast](https://www.youtube.com/@the_bitesize_japanese_podcast)

## **あかね的日本語教室**

博主就是在日本语学校教学的老师，内容还是很多的，语法教学。 各种场景下的真实对话，比如咖啡店点餐，酒店入住等等。

Welcome to Akane’s Japanese class!!  
Hello, my name's Akane and I'm a Japanese teacher.  
My videos are meant for intermediate and advanced learners but if you're a beginner, that's perfectly OK!  
Because all my content is in Japanese, you'll get used to the language faster and improve your listening skills.  
You'll also learn useful, everyday phrases that will help you make new Japanese friends, pass job interviews, and much more.  
Don't forget to subscribe, thank you!  
(English translation by Eric @e\_\_vibe)

こんにちは！あかねです。  
私は日本人です。  
日本語学校で日本語を教えている日本語教師です。  
日本語を勉強している人や、日本に興味がある人のために動画を投稿しています。  
このチャンネルが少しでもみなさんの役に立ったらうれしいです。

[あかね的日本語教室](https://www.youtube.com/@Akane-JapaneseClass)
